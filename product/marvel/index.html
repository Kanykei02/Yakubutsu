    The Marvel Cinematic Universe (abbreviated KVM) is a fictional universe, an American media franchise, a series of superhero films based on Marvel comics and developed by Marvel Studios. The universe was created by combining several films and TV series with common actors, characters and events into a common storyline.

    “It's scary and fun at the same time. It is something that no one has ever done before, and it is because of this spirit of novelty that everyone enthusiastically embraces this approach. Other directors are not used to bringing in already engaged actors from other films, tying to certain storylines or locations, but I think ... everyone ended up agreeing to it and thought it was fun. Mainly because we have consistently maintained that the film we are currently working on is above all else. All of our “bundles” are all fun and will also be very important if you want to. If fans want to look further and see a deeper connection, it will be there. There are also some obvious "links" that, we hope, the main audience will be able to follow. But ... the reason all directors have agreed to take part in this venture is because their films have to be competitive in their own right. They need a fresh perspective, a unique tone, and the fact that they can be interconnected if the viewer wants to follow these breadcrumbs is a bonus. "

    The story-driven campaign is one of the best parts of Marvel's Avengers. During its passage, the work of developers is noticeable, who tried to implement a variety of entertainment for the players. At the center of the story is Kamala Khan, aka Miss Marvel. The girl was invited to the "Avengers Day" in San Francisco, as one of the finalists of the competition of works dedicated to the famous superhero team. However, in the midst of the holiday, an explosion occurred on the Golden Gate Bridge. A group of villains led by Taskmaster used weapons stolen from Stark Industries and staged a terrorist attack. The confrontation ended sadly: Captain America died, and the revolutionary reactor, which the Avengers wanted to present to the public, exploded. The metropolis was covered with a powerful energy wave, which turned it into an exclusion zone. As a result of the incident, the residents of San Francisco began to suffer from a strange disease. They immersed themselves in cocoons, and emerged from them as owners of supernatural abilities.

    Avengers is an action-adventure game. The game has a cooperative mode, which is designed for 4 people with the ability to customize and develop various heroes. Iron Man, Captain America, Thor, Black Widow, Hulk and Miss Marvel will be available as playable characters at the time of the game's release.
